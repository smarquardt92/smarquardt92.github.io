A Quick Background


Sarah Marquardt

I come from an engineering and sales background. During college, I developed an impressive set of soft skills as a sales manager for a residential painting company. Post-college, I spent some time as a field engineer in the oil and gas industry, where I developed real-world technical skills and a tolerance for gas station burritos (some aren't that bad). Soon after, I decided to change my career trajectory and find something that better matched my personality and would satisfy my constant need to learn. The dynamic world of data is a perfect match for me. I'm looking to break into the industry after graduating from UT Austin's data analysis and visualization program. With a passion for coding and problem solving, I look forward to the challenges this new field has in store.
